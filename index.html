Updates

Possible 3 groups for war

Posted on June 20, 2016

Over the next 3 AQs, we expect members to follow the roster for AQs.

If everyone follows the roster well and each group explores most/all the map, then we plan on opening up war to 3 groups, so everyone can have the chance to win war rewards.

However, this depends on the cooperation of everyone in the alliance. If we find that there is not good participation and that members aren’t following the roster, then we will stay with 2 groups for wars until otherwise.

Thank you.

Website Up and Running

Posted on June 15, 2016

Today we are releasing our new website. http://tiny.cc/coulsonmcoc

From here, players can find rules, updates, spreadsheets, and helpful resources to become stronger.

We will also now be implementing a roster, where certain players will have designated spots for certain groups in alliance wars/quests.

To find out which group you are assigned to, simply go to the Roster page and find your name on the list. We expect all members to follow this roster. Those who continually don’t will be kicked.

Notice that in Alliance Wars, we have given some of our top veteran members reserved spots in each group. All other spots are open to other players. This is because currently, we only do 2 groups for war.

However, if players follow the roster and do well in AQs, then we hope to open 3 groups for war in the future.

Some new changes

Posted on June 8, 2016

Due to the fact that we have not been reaching the minimum requirements for Summoner Advancement, and have therefor suffered by not receiving our weekly dose of 4* shards, the officers and I have decided that we will now be doing 3 wars (2 groups) a week to make up for it.

We also plan on doing AQ 3 once a week, AQ 2 while not in war (or in placement phase), and AQ 1 during war.

We also plan on implementing a roster sometime in the future to better improve our efficiency in completing AQ and AW. So certain players will be assigned to certain groups in AQ and AW. But that is still in the works.

/pWe know these are quite a few changes, so if you have any questions, just ask.

Basic Info

Weekly Schedule

  • Alliance War 3 times a week
  • Alliance Quest 3 once a week
  • Alliance Quest 2 while not in war
  • Alliance Quest 1 during war

Requirements

  • Level 40
  • 4 4 stars at 3/30
  • PI rating of 40k
  • 40 champions

What We Expect

  • Participate in Alliance Quests
  • If you join war, you must announce your path and stick with it
  • PI rating of 40k
  • Participate in Alliance Events
  • Must have good communication with officers/leader when necessary
  • If you're going to be gone, out of town, on vacation, etc, let us know so we don't kick you

Reasons for kicking

  • Not participating in 3/5 Alliance Quests
  • Taking an open slot in Alliance wars and not participating
  • Not following/announcing paths in Alliance Wars
  • Conitunually failing to follow instructions
